If you stop paying child maintenance

If a payment is more than 5 working days late, we can take enforcement action.

If you miss a Direct Pay payment, we will follow the steps below.

  1. A caseworker will contact you to find out why you have not paid.
  2. We will arrange for you to pay what you owe.
  3. We will warn you about action that might be taken if you do not pay.
  4. We will take action to get child maintenance owed if you do not respond.
We will tell you more about this after you send your application.
