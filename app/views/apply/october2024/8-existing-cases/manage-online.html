Why do I need an online account?
You can use your online account to manage your application. You will be able to:
  • see when payments are due
  • tell us if any payments have been missed
  • contact us about your application
  • ask us a question via webchat which is available on payment and report a change screens
  • update your contact details
  • tell us about changes to your circumstances
  • tell us if you have any additional expenses or income
What if I already have an online account?
You still need to create a PIN so that information about this application and any previous cases will be available in the same account.
