Using a child maintenance account

The online child maintenance account is a secure way to get updates about this application. Nobody else can access your account.

You can:

  • see when payments are due
  • tell us if any payments have been missed
  • update your contact details
  • tell us about changes to your circumstances

If you have another child maintenance case

You may have a child maintenance account if you already pay or receive child maintenance. You can use the same account to manage all your cases and applications in one place.
