Tell us about your children

To calculate your maintenance properly, we need to get some information about your children.

First, we need to know about the child or children you’re applying to include in this maintenance arrangement.

Then we also need to know if you have any other children who you pay maintenance for, if it’s a private arrangement (not using Child Maintenance Service).

Finally, we need to know if any other children live with you the majority of the time (like a partner’s children).

We’ll ask about one child at a time, and you can keep adding children to your application until you have added them all.

All children must be under the age of 20.
