What is your child's full name?

This should be a child who you accept parentage for, and who you’re applying to pay maintenance for in this arrangement.

If there is more than one child for this arrangement, we’ll ask about them in the next steps.
What is parentage?

Parentage is another way to say “a child’s legally recognised parents.”

This can include:

  • a biological parent (unless the child has been adopted)
  • a parent by adoption
  • a parent under a parental order (used in surrogacy case)
If you're unsure whether you have an open case for your child
Sign in to your online child maintenance account to check if your case is still open. If you cannot sign in to your account, contact Child Maintenance Service.
